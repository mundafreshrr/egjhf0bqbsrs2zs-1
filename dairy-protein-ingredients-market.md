<p><p>The dairy protein ingredients market encompasses various types, including milk protein concentrates and isolates, which offer a balanced amino acid profile and are used in nutritional products. Whey protein concentrates and isolates provide high digestibility and are favored in sports nutrition. Whey protein hydrolysates offer pre-digested forms for quicker absorption, ideal for post-workout recovery. Casein and caseinates provide slow-digesting proteins, suitable for sustained amino acid release. Each type caters to different dietary needs and functional applications in food and beverages.</p></p>

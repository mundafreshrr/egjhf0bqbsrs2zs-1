<p><ul><li>Sequential Type</li><li>Simultaneous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICP-OES spectrometer market is categorized into two main types: sequential and simultaneous. Sequential type ICP-OES instruments measure one wavelength at a time, providing high sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for specific applications requiring detailed analysis. In contrast, simultaneous type instruments analyze multiple wavelengths simultaneously, allowing for faster analysis and increased throughput, which is advantageous in high-volume laboratories. Both types serve diverse industries, including environmental monitoring and material analysis, addressing varying analytical needs and demands.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Toasted Malted Wheat Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li><li>Non-Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toasted malted wheat flour market is divided into two main types: diastatic and non-diastatic. Diastatic toasted malted wheat flour contains active enzymes that help in the breakdown of starches into sugars during fermentation, enhancing the flavor and texture of baked goods. In contrast, non-diastatic toasted malted wheat flour is roasted to develop flavor but lacks these enzymes, making it suitable for recipes where enzymatic activity is not required. Both types are utilized in various food products for their unique characteristics.</p></p>

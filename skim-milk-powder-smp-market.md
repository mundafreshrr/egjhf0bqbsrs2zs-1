<p><ul><li>Low Heat SMP</li><li>Medium Heat SMP</li><li>High Heat SMP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skim milk powder (SMP) market is categorized into three main types based on heat treatment: Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP is produced at a lower temperature, retaining more protein and nutritional quality, making it ideal for infant formulas and dairy products. Medium Heat SMP undergoes moderate heating, providing a balance between solubility and shelf stability, suitable for bakery products. High Heat SMP is processed at high temperatures, enhancing shelf life but reducing functional properties, often used in confectionery and desserts.</p></p>

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cameras market includes two primary types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture two-dimensional images by taking a snapshot of a scene, making them ideal for applications requiring full-frame captures, such as quality inspection and identification. In contrast, line scan cameras acquire images one line at a time, suitable for continuous processes like web inspection or conveyor systems. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on the specific imaging requirements and operational environments.</p></p>

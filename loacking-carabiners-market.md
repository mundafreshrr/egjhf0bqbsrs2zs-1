<p><p>The locking carabiners market is categorized into three main types: straight gate, bent gate, and wire gate carabiners. Straight gate carabiners feature a linear opening for direct attachment, ideal for quick connects in climbing and rigging. Bent gate carabiners have a curved gate, facilitating easier clipping, particularly with slings or cord. Wire gate carabiners utilize a lightweight design with a flexible gate, minimizing freezing and improving security during active use. Each type serves distinct preferences and applications in climbing and outdoor activities.</p></p>
